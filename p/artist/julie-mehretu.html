Julie Mehretu

b. 1970 · Maps, riots and weather systems — abstraction at stadium scale

Born in Addis Ababa to an Ethiopian geography professor and an American Montessori teacher, Mehretu fled with her family in 1977 as the Derg regime closed in, growing up in East Lansing, Michigan.

Her canvases laminate strata — architectural plans, stadium diagrams, migration routes, news photographs blurred to weather — under storms of inked gesture, building what she calls 'story maps of no location.' Mural, her 25-metre commission for Goldman Sachs's lobby, compresses the history of capitalism into a single weather system; HOWL, eon, her two-canvas epic for SFMOMA, does it for the American West.
