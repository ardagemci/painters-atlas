Shen Zhou

1427–1509 · Master of the Wu School — mountains painted between cups of tea

Born into a scholarly landowning family in Suzhou, Shen Zhou declined the examination path to office — citing his duty to his ageing mother — and spent eighty-two years exactly where he wished: among gardens, poems and friends.

His ink landscapes carry a companionable warmth rare in monumental painting: a tiny poet on a cliff faces the void, and the void feels friendly.
