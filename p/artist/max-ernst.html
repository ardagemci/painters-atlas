Max Ernst

1891–1976 · Bird-man of the unconscious — rubbing, scraping and gluing dreams loose

A Cologne philosophy student gassed and shelled in WWI — he listed his death and rebirth in his autobiography — Ernst founded Cologne Dada, then jumped to Paris Surrealism.

Ernst was Surrealism's chief engineer of accident: collage novels stitched from Victorian engravings (Une Semaine de Bonté), frottage rubbings that turned floorboards into forests, grattage, decalcomania — each a machine for outwitting intention.
