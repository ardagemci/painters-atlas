David Hockney

b. 1937 · A bigger splash — from Bradford to LA pools to iPad springs

A Bradford clerk's son who announced his vocation at eleven, Hockney left for California in 1964 and found his great subject: sunlight on water and on tanned Los Angeles leisure.

A Bigger Splash froze a dive's aftermath into acrylic flatness; Portrait of an Artist set a living-artist auction record of $90.3 million in 2018.
