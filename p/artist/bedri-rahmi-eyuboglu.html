Bedri Rahmi Eyüboğlu

1911–1975 · Kilim motifs meet Matisse — painter-poet of the Anatolian eye

Born in Görele on the Black Sea to a governor-poet father, Eyüboğlu trained in Istanbul and under André Lhote in Paris, married the Romanian-born painter Eren, and taught at the Istanbul academy for four decades.

His credo was a marriage: folk art's eye — kilims, embroidery, wooden spoons, çini — with modern colour's appetite.
