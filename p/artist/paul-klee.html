Paul Klee

1879–1940 · Taking a line for a walk — wit, colour and ten thousand small miracles

Born near Bern to a German music teacher and a Swiss singer, Klee was a violinist good enough for the municipal orchestra before choosing the slower art.

Klee built a private universe at postcard scale: fish, moons, arrows, twittering birds cranked by a handle, grids of colour like irrigated fields.
