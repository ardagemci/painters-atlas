Hannah Höch

1889–1978 · Cut with the kitchen knife — Berlin Dada's only woman, and its sharpest blade

Trained in Berlin's applied arts schools, Höch worked a decade at the Ullstein press empire designing handicraft patterns — and quietly pocketing the photo archives that became her ammunition.

Her giant photomontage Cut with the Kitchen Knife shredded Weimar's generals, dadaists and dancers into one whirling indictment — the medium's founding masterpiece.
