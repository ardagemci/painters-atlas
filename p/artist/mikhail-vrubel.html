Mikhail Vrubel

1856–1910 · The Demon in crystal — facets a generation before Cubism

Omsk-born, classically trained, Vrubel restored Byzantine frescoes in Kyiv before Moscow's merchant-patrons adopted him.

He built form from crystalline facets — wings like shattered amethyst, faces assembled from prisms — a private geometry that anticipates Cubism by fifteen years and earned only mockery at first.
