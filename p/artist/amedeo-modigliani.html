Amedeo Modigliani

1884–1920 · Swan necks and almond eyes — Montparnasse's doomed prince

Born to a Sephardic Jewish family in Livorno already bankrupt at his birth, 'Modì' arrived in Paris in 1906 — elegant, tubercular, reciting Dante.

His mature style is instantly his: necks like columns, faces like masks, eyes often blank almonds — 'When I know your soul, I will paint your eyes,' he reportedly told a sitter.
