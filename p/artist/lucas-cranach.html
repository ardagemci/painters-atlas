Lucas Cranach the Elder

1472–1553 · The Reformation's image machine — and Luther's best man

Court painter to three Electors of Saxony in Wittenberg, Cranach was also the town's richest citizen: pharmacist, wine merchant, print publisher, thrice mayor.

His workshop was a production line of genius: slinky Venuses in feathered hats, serpent-signed portraits of Luther by the dozen (the Reformation's official face), hunting scenes and altarpieces — over a thousand surviving works.
