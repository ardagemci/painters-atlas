Marcel Duchamp

1887–1968 · The man who signed a urinal — and changed the question forever

Born into a Normandy family that produced four artists, Duchamp painted his way through Impressionism and Cubism by thirty — then largely stopped, preferring chess, puns and ideas.

Nude Descending a Staircase scandalised the 1913 Armory Show ('an explosion in a shingle factory'); four years later Fountain — a porcelain urinal signed 'R.
