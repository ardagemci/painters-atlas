J.-A.-D. Ingres

1780–1867 · Line above all — perfection with a deliberately extra vertebra

Born in Montauban, trained under David, Ingres spent long years in Italy nursing genius and grievances in equal measure.

'Drawing is the probity of art,' he decreed, then cheerfully broke anatomy whenever beauty demanded: the Grande Odalisque's back has extra vertebrae, necks swan beyond nature.
