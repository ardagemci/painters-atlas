Lavinia Fontana

1552–1614 · Bologna's professional — the first woman to run the full game

Daughter of a Bolognese painter, Lavinia Fontana turned her father's modest workshop into her own thriving practice — the first woman in Europe to build a full professional career as a painter, with public commissions, papal patrons and market-rate prices.

Bologna's noblewomen queued for her portraits, trusting her with their jewels and their faces alike; her fame carried her to Rome under Pope Paul V.
