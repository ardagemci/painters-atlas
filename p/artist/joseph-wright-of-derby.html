Joseph Wright of Derby

1734–1797 · Candlelight for the age of science

Wright stayed loyal to Derby — England's industrial crucible — while London beckoned, painting the scientists, mill-owners and experiments of the Midlands Enlightenment.

He transplanted Caravaggio's candlelight from saints to science: a travelling lecturer suffocating a cockatoo in a vacuum jar while a family watches — wonder, doubt and a little girl's grief in one pool of lamplight.
