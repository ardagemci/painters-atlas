Banksy

b. circa 1974 · Identity unknown, message unmissable — the stencil as global headline

Presumed born in Bristol around 1974, Banksy emerged from the city's 1990s graffiti scene and has kept his identity unconfirmed through three decades of global fame — an anonymity that is itself his most durable artwork.

His stencils — rats with paintbrushes, a protester hurling flowers, a girl losing a heart balloon — fuse one-liner speed with political bite.
