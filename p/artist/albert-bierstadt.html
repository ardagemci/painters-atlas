Albert Bierstadt

1830–1902 · The Rockies at opera scale — tickets at the door

Born in Solingen and raised in Massachusetts, Bierstadt joined survey expeditions west in 1859 and 1863, sketching the Rockies, Yosemite and Oregon Trail.

His mountain panoramas — six feet by ten of golden light, waterfalls and improbable peaks — were exhibited as events: darkened rooms, curtains, admission fees, opera glasses recommended.
