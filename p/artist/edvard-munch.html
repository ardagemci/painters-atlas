Edvard Munch

1863–1944 · The scream heard round the world

Munch painted states of mind as if they were weather — jealousy, illness and dread given color and a horizon. The Scream became modern anxiety's logo, but the whole Frieze of Life pulses with the same voltage. German Expressionism is his echo chamber.

The Scream — a figure on a bridge under a blood sky, 'a scream passing through nature' — became modern anxiety's logo, one panel of his lifelong cycle The Frieze of Life: love, jealousy, illness, death, repainted in versions for decades.
