Caravaggio

1571–1610 · Saints with dirty feet, painted by a man on the run

Caravaggio detonated European painting in one decade: street kids as angels, pilgrims with dirty feet, miracles staged in cellar darkness under a single brutal light. Every noir shadow since — Rembrandt's, Ribera's, cinema's — runs on his electricity. He painted saints the way police light suspects.
