John Constable

1776–1837 · Six feet of Suffolk sky — landscape as autobiography

A prosperous miller's son from East Bergholt, Constable spent seven years courting Maria Bicknell against her family's opposition, marrying her only after inheriting independence.

Constable painted one square mile of boyhood — mills, locks, towpaths — with revolutionary fidelity: flickering whites, real clouds, dung and dew.
