Nakkaş Osman

fl. 1560–1595 · The sultan's chief eye — an empire documented in gold and lapis

Head of the imperial nakkaşhane — the palace painting workshop — under three sultans, Osman is the classical Ottoman miniature's defining master.

His workshop's Surname-i Hümayun records the 1582 circumcision festival of Prince Mehmed — fifty-two days of guild parades, acrobats, fireworks and confectionery palaces marching across hundreds of double pages: the Ottoman sixteenth century filmed in gouache.
