Titian

c. 1488–1576 · Venice's emperor of colour, painter to actual emperors

Born Tiziano Vecellio in the Dolomite town of Pieve di Cadore, he came to Venice as a boy and ruled its painting for sixty years.

His thunderous Assumption made him Venice's official painter at thirty; Holy Roman Emperor Charles V made him court painter, knight and count — legend says the emperor once stooped to pick up Titian's dropped brush.
