Şeker Ahmed Paşa

1841–1907 · 'Sugar' Ahmed — the gentle Paşa who brought the easel to Istanbul

Sent to Paris by Sultan Abdülaziz to study painting under Gérôme and Boulanger, Ahmed Ali returned to serve the palace as court officer, protocol master and tireless evangelist for oil painting — so sweet-tempered that the nickname 'Şeker' (Sugar) replaced his name.

In 1873 he organised what is regarded as Istanbul's first public group painting exhibition — the founding act of the Turkish gallery tradition.
