Jean-Michel Basquiat

1960–1988 · SAMO© to the auction block — crowns, anatomy and eight furious years

Born in Brooklyn to a Haitian father and Puerto Rican mother, Basquiat was a gifted, restless kid whose mother took him to museums and whose hospital stay at seven (hit by a car) came with a gift of Gray's Anatomy — a lifelong source.

As SAMO©, with Al Diaz, he aphorised SoHo's walls; by 1981 the 'Radiant Child' essay in Artforum announced the gallery phase.
