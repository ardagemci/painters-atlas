Francisco de Zurbarán

1598–1664 · Monks and lemons in God's spotlight

From Extremadura, Zurbarán became Seville's painter of the cloister: monastery cycles for Dominicans, Mercedarians, Carthusians — white habits against darkness.

He painted fabric the way mystics pray: the hanging weight of Saint Serapion's robes is as devotional as the martyr's face.
