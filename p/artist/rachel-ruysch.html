Rachel Ruysch

1664–1750 · Sixty years of impossible bouquets

Daughter of Amsterdam's famed anatomist-botanist Frederik Ruysch — whose curiosity cabinets she helped decorate as a girl — Ruysch painted from fifteen to eighty-five, through marriage and ten children.

Her bouquets are botanically impossible — spring tulips beside autumn grapes — and optically irresistible: asymmetric spills of blossom alive with caterpillars, beetles and dew.
