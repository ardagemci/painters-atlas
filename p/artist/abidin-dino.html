Abidin Dino

1913–1993 · 'Can you paint happiness, Abidin?' — the line a poet hung on him forever

Born into a cosmopolitan Istanbul family, Dino co-founded Turkey's modernist 'd Group' at twenty, drew for Soviet film studios in Leningrad, and was internally exiled to Anatolia in the 1940s for his politics — sketching cotton workers in Adana.

A draughtsman above all — hands clasped, folded, pleading, multiplied across decades; flowers drawn in single unbroken lines; torture documented in ink when words were dangerous.
