John Everett Millais

1829–1896 · Ophelia in the bathtub — Victorian England's prodigy

The youngest student ever admitted to the Royal Academy schools — aged eleven — Millais co-founded the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood at nineteen.

Ophelia — painted petal by petal over a Surrey river, then over Elizabeth Siddal in a tin bath of cooling water — is Victorian art's most loved picture and a botanist's field guide.
