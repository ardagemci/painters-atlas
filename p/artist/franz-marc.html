Franz Marc

1880–1916 · Blue horses, golden cows — animals as the souls we lost

A Munich philosophy student turned painter, Marc co-founded Der Blaue Reiter with Kandinsky in 1911, convinced animals saw the world more purely than corrupted mankind.

His colour was theology: blue for the spiritual and male, yellow for gentle joy, red for the violence of matter — deer, foxes and above all horses painted as creatures of pure feeling.
