Vasily Surikov

1848–1916 · Russian history, painted from inside the crowd

Born to a Cossack family in Siberian Krasnoyarsk — he claimed the seventeenth century was still alive there, and painted like he meant it.

His great trilogy — the Streltsy rebels awaiting execution, the disgraced Menshikov in exile, the Old Believer martyr Morozova hauled through the snow on a sledge — made the crowd the protagonist of history painting: every onlooker a biography, every winter authentic.
