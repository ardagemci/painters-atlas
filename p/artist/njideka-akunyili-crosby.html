Njideka Akunyili Crosby

b. 1983 · Lagos and Los Angeles on one surface — intimacy, layered like memory

Born in Enugu, Nigeria — her mother, Dora Akunyili, became a national hero battling counterfeit drugs as a regulator — Akunyili Crosby came to the US at sixteen, studied at Swarthmore and Yale, and married American artist Justin Crosby.

Her large-scale interiors — couples at tea, families on patterned sofas, her own wedding — are built from paint, coloured pencil and acetone photo-transfers of Nigerian magazines, family albums and fabric, so every shadow teems with imagery.
