Francis Bacon

1909–1992 · Screaming popes and glass cages — the nervous system painted raw

Born in Dublin to an English horse-trainer father who horsewhipped him and expelled him at sixteen over his sexuality, Bacon drifted through Berlin and Paris into London's Soho, where he held court for decades at the Colony Room between gambling sprees.

His 1944 Three Studies — writhing furies on orange grounds — announced a post-war art of the scream.
