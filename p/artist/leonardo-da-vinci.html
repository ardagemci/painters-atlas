Leonardo da Vinci

1452–1519 · The universal mind who painted the world's most famous smile

Leonardo made painting a form of research: light studied like physics, faces like anatomy, landscapes like geology. Sfumato — modelling without lines — gave images an inner weather no one had achieved before. Every painter who ever blurred an edge to suggest a soul is quoting him.

Leonardo finished perhaps fifteen paintings in fifty years — and changed painting forever with them.
