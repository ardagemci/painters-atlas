Jacob van Ruisdael

c. 1629–1682 · Two-thirds sky — the landscape learns to brood

Born into a Haarlem family of frame-makers and painters, Ruisdael never married, caring for his father into old age.

Ruisdael made Dutch flatness monumental: towering cloudbanks over tiny human business, a windmill standing against weather like a fate.
