P.S. Krøyer

1851–1909 · The blue hour, bottled at Skagen

Born in Norway, raised by foster parents in Copenhagen after his mother was judged unable to care for him — a prodigy admitted to the academy at nine.

Krøyer owned the blue hour: that northern moment when sea and sky share one pigment and figures in white seem lit from inside.
