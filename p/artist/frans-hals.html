Frans Hals

c. 1582–1666 · The fastest smile in the Dutch Golden Age

Born in Antwerp, raised in Haarlem after his family fled the Spanish, Hals spent his life in that one city — painting its militias, merchants, fishwives and drinkers.

Hals painted speed itself: slashing, visible strokes that snap into silk, lace and grins at viewing distance.
