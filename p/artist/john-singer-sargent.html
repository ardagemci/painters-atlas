John Singer Sargent

1856–1925 · The last grand portraitist — silk by the yard, in single strokes

Born in Florence to nomadic American expatriates, Sargent grew up multilingual in hotels and museums.

Sargent could state a satin sleeve in one loaded stroke — bravura descended from Velázquez and Hals that made duchesses queue for years.
