Jan Steen

c. 1626–1679 · 'A Jan Steen household' — chaos, painted with love

Born in Leiden to a family of brewers, Steen ran taverns and a brewery alongside painting — research, one suspects.

His crowded households — children smoking, dogs on the table, grandmothers cackling, the moral unravelling in real time — made his name a Dutch proverb for happy domestic chaos.
