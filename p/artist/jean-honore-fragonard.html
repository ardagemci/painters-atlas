Jean-Honoré Fragonard

1732–1806 · Painted mischief at full speed — then history pulled the rope

Born in Grasse, the perfume town, Fragonard trained under Chardin briefly and Boucher decisively, won the Prix de Rome, and chose private pleasure-painting over an official career.

The Swing says it all: a slipper flying, a hidden lover gazing up silk skirts, a bishop unwittingly pulling the rope — frivolity executed with brushwork so alive it outclasses the joke.
