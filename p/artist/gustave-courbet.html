Gustave Courbet

1819–1877 · 'Show me an angel and I'll paint one' — Realism's battering ram

A vintner's son from Ornans in the Franche-Comté, Courbet stormed Paris with provincial confidence the size of his canvases.

A Burial at Ornans put fifty life-size villagers around an ordinary grave on the scale reserved for kings and gods — 'the burial of Romanticism,' critics fumed.
