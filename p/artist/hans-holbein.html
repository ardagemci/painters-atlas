Hans Holbein the Younger

c. 1497–1543 · The cool eye of the Tudor court

Born in Augsburg into a family of painters, Holbein worked in Basel until the Reformation's iconoclasm dried up commissions.

Holbein's portraits are almost frightening in their precision: Erasmus at his desk, Thomas More in velvet, Henry VIII as a wall of menace.
