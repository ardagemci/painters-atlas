Suzanne Valadon

1865–1938 · From trapeze to easel — the model who out-painted her painters

Born to an unmarried laundress in Montmartre, Valadon was a circus acrobat until a trapeze fall at fifteen, then the neighbourhood's favourite model — for Puvis, Renoir, Toulouse-Lautrec — absorbing technique from the other side of the easel.

Degas saw her drawings and bought them — 'you are one of us' — teaching her etching and pushing her into exhibition.
