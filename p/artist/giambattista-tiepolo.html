Giovanni Battista Tiepolo

1696–1770 · The last great ceiling painter — skies without end

Venetian by birth and temperament, Tiepolo rose from a shipping merchant's family to become Europe's most sought-after fresco painter, working with his painter sons Giandomenico and Lorenzo as a flying squad of illusionists.

Above the Würzburg Residenz staircase he painted the largest fresco in the world: four continents swirling around Apollo in a sky that seems to dissolve the architecture.
