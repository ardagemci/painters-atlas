Zinaida Serebriakova

1884–1967 · At the dressing table — radiance painted on the eve of catastrophe

Born into the Benois dynasty of artists on a Ukrainian estate, Serebriakova painted her luminous self-portrait at twenty-five and woke up famous.

At the Dressing-Table — a young woman combing her hair in candid morning happiness — was bought by the Tretyakov within months and remains among Russia's most loved paintings.
