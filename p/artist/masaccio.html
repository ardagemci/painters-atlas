Masaccio

1401–1428 · Perspective's big bang — dead at twenty-six, copied for centuries

Tommaso di Ser Giovanni earned the nickname Masaccio — 'clumsy Tom' — for caring about everything except appearances and accounts.

His Holy Trinity in Santa Maria Novella opens the church wall into the first rigorously constructed perspective space in Western art — Florentines queued, Vasari says, as if at a hole punched in the wall.
